Detailed Description

Standard handlers for ISM3 driver callbacks.

Author
marc..nosp@m.leem.nosp@m.ann@m.nosp@m.aste.nosp@m.r.hes.nosp@m.-so..nosp@m.ch

See ISM3 sync modes and ISM3 states for complementary explanations.

These handlers are not mandatory, but why not use them now that they're here?

Do not modify headers unless they conform to definitions in ism3.h
